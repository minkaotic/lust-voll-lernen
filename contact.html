Interested in joining us?

We practice every Monday 6:30pm around the corner from the Honor Oak Pub.

We accept new members on a rolling basis, but if you have no previous experience in morris dancing, the best time to join us is around September: this is the end of the busy morris season, and will give us more time to support and teach complete newcomers to the dance.

If you're interested, email quaggymorris@gmail.com to arrange joining us for a trial practice!

Book us for events!

We dance at many local events, including festivals, private parties, or just outside all manner of pubs. We're most active in South East London, around the borough of Lewisham, but will travel further afield to spread the morris joy.

If you would like to book us for your event, email quaggymorris@gmail.com and we'll work out the details together.
